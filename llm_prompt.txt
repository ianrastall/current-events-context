Create a deep-research global news report for a single day. 

PHASE 1: RESEARCH
Conduct a broad global news sweep for the date given in INPUTS.
Identify the day's biggest events worldwide using strong, high-confidence sources.
Deliberately check for major developments in: North America, Latin America and the Caribbean, Europe, Middle East and North Africa, Sub-Saharan Africa, South Asia, East Asia, Southeast Asia, and Oceania.
Do not force equal regional representation, but actively check for major events that may be underrepresented in Western coverage.

PHASE 2: OUTPUT
Output a highly structured Markdown report. Do NOT output YAML. 
For each event (aim for 12 to 18 major events), provide the following strictly formatted sections. Do not deviate from these headers:

## Event: [Headline]
* **Category & Subcategory:** [e.g., Armed conflicts and attacks / Drone strikes]
* **Geography:** [Countries], [Places]
* **Summary:** A robust, substantial 1-paragraph summary.
* **What Happened:** Exactly 3 to 5 highly specific bullet points.
* **Why It Matters:** 1 to 3 bullet points explaining broader significance.
* **Actors:** * Primary: [Only if explicitly supported by sources]
    * Secondary: [If applicable]
* **Casualties/Damage:** [Killed: X, Injured: X, Displaced: X, Arrest Count: X, Damage Descriptions. Use "Unknown" if genuinely unavailable.]
* **Sources:** [For each source, provide: Outlet Name, URL, Publication Date, and a brief list of claims this specific source supports. Prefer 2 to 5 direct, high-confidence sources per event.]

FACT RULES:
- Be factual, neutral, and source-grounded. Do not guess.
- Merge duplicate reporting about the same real-world event into one event section.
- Do not pad with background articles or explainers.

INPUTS:
DATE_ISO = [insert date]
